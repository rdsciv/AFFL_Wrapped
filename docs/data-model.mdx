## Public story contract

Each season object contains:

- champion, runner-up, and title game;
- league pulse and activity totals;
- all-play power standings and luck leaders;
- four matchup awards;
- season and single-game player impact;
- draft and undrafted spotlights where supported;
- one summary card per team;
- advanced management, roster-origin, injury, age, upset, honor, and pickup graphs where supported;
- official activity counters plus event-level acquisition value;
- explicit coverage metadata.

## Provenance limits

ESPN does not return complete player-level draft and event-level transaction history before 2018. Older annuals still include validated standings, scores, lineups, acquisition counters, and team outcomes. Fantasy Genius Wrapped enrichment is available for 2020-2025. The interface labels narrower source coverage instead of presenting inferred events as facts.

The retrieved ESPN event feed contains free-agent and waiver events but no direct trade event type. Official trade totals remain available through team counters; possible roster-to-roster player movements are displayed separately with a 75% confidence label.

One 2016 season aggregate differs from ESPN's internal display by nine points; the source snapshots and normalized calculations are retained and documented in the private validation archive.
