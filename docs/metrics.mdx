## All-play expected wins

For each team-week, compare that team's score with every other score in the league:

```text
weekly expected wins = (scores beaten + 0.5 x scores tied) / (teams - 1)
season expected wins = sum(weekly expected wins)
```

This expresses schedule-neutral strength on the same scale as actual wins.

## Luck

```text
luck = actual wins - all-play expected wins
```

Positive values indicate a team converted its weekly scoring into more wins than expected. Negative values indicate the opposite. Luck is descriptive, not causal: it captures schedule outcomes, not managerial skill.

## Matchup awards

- **Biggest Blowout:** largest absolute score margin.
- **Closest Nail-Biter:** smallest absolute score margin.
- **Scoreboard Meltdown:** largest combined matchup score.
- **Pillow Fight:** smallest positive combined matchup score.

## Carried the team

```text
carry share = player season points for team / team points for
```

The award identifies the largest player share of a team's official season scoring total.

## Draft and waiver spotlights

Draft spotlights rank drafted players by recorded season scoring. Undrafted impact identifies high-scoring player IDs absent from that season's draft table. These modules appear only when the source coverage supports the distinction.

## Expected fantasy points added

```text
xFP added (4W) = sum of ESPN weekly projections during the first four scoring periods after an executed add
```

The window stops when the player leaves the destination roster. Actual starter points use the same scoring periods and exclude bench and injured-reserve slots. A fixed horizon keeps early- and late-season pickups comparable.

Projection coverage is always reported. Missing weekly projections remain null and are excluded from xFP totals rather than converted to zero.

## Transaction grains

- **Official adds, drops, and trades** are season-level ESPN team counters for 2014-2025.
- **Executed add/drop items** are event-level ESPN records available from 2018 onward.
- **Inferred player movements** are direct roster-to-roster snapshot transitions at 75% confidence. They are displayed separately and are not labeled as official trades.

Official counters and event items answer different questions, so their totals are not expected to reconcile one-for-one in every season.
