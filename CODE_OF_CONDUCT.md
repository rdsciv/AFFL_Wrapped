# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

AFFL Wrapped is a place for curious, constructive work. Be respectful, assume good intent, welcome newcomers, critique ideas rather than people, and make room for perspectives beyond your own.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats, doxxing, sexualized attention, sustained disruption, or publishing private league data without permission.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, code review, and any public space where someone is representing this project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Reports can be made privately through a [GitHub security advisory](https://github.com/rdsciv/AFFL_Wrapped/security/advisories/new). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and GitHub Community Guidelines, adapted for this project's size and available reporting channels.
